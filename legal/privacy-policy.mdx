**Last Updated: January 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how Dubs ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our decentralized sports betting platform and related services (collectively, the "Services").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Wallet Information**
- Public wallet address (required to use the platform)
- Transaction signatures for authentication

**Profile Information**
- Username (chosen by you)
- Avatar/profile picture
- Email address (optional)
- Referral codes used or generated

**Communication Data**
- Chat messages sent through our platform
- Direct messages to other users
- Support inquiries

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Information**
- Games created, joined, and outcomes
- Betting history and transaction records
- Features used and interactions with the platform

**Device Information**
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device identifiers
- IP address

**Blockchain Data**
- All transactions on the Solana blockchain are public
- This includes bet amounts, game participation, and payouts

## Blockchain Transparency

<Warning>
**Important:** Dubs operates on the Solana blockchain. All betting transactions, including wallet addresses, bet amounts, and game outcomes, are publicly visible on the blockchain. This is fundamental to how decentralized applications work.
</Warning>

What's public on the blockchain:
- Your wallet address
- Bet amounts and game participation
- Payouts and transactions
- Transaction timestamps

What's NOT on the blockchain:
- Your email address
- Your chosen username (stored in our database)
- Private chat messages
- Your IP address

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure database storage
- Access controls for employee access
- Regular security assessments

<Info>
Your funds are secured by the Solana blockchain and your wallet's private keys. We never have access to your private keys or the ability to move your funds without your signed approval.
</Info>
